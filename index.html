Biography

  • I was born in 1992, Xi'an, China.

  • I received the B.S. degree in automation from North China Electric Power University, China, in 2014.

  • I received the M.E. degree in control engineering from Xidian University, Xi’an China, in 2017.

  • Since 2017, I am engaged in a Ph.D. co-tutorship between University of Cagliari, Italy (Electronics and Computer Engineering) and Xidian University, China (Control Theory and Control Engineering), co-supervised by Prof. Zhiwu Li (Xidian University) and Prof. Alessandro Giua (University of Cagliari)
