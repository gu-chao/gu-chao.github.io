Biography

  • I was born in Xi’an in February 12, 1992.

  • I received the B.S. degree in automation from North China Electric Power University, China, in 2014.

  • I received the M.E. degree in control engineering from Xidian University, Xi’an China, in 2017.

  • Since 2017, I am engaged in a Ph.D. co-tutorship between University of Cagliari, Italy (Electronics and Computer Engineering) and Xidian University, China (Control Theory and Control Engineering).

Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • Petri Net Theory and Its Applications

  • Discrete Event Systems

  • Supervisory Control Theory

  • Security Problems in Cyber-Physical Systems
